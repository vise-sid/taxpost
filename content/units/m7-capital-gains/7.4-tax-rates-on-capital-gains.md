# Unit 7.4: Tax Rates on Capital Gains (Sections 196-198)

## Overview

The three rate sections — 111A, 112, and 112A — are renumbered to Sections 196, 197, and 198. The rates themselves were changed by Finance (No.2) Act 2024 (effective 23 July 2024) and are carried forward without modification. The genuine 2025 Act story in this unit is about what **did not survive**: the LTCL-vs-STCG one-time set-off proposed in the Income Tax Bill 2025 was **withdrawn** in the final Act.

> Rates, exemption thresholds, and grandfathering provisions as amended by Finance (No.2) Act 2024 are carried forward — no change by the 2025 Act.

---

## Old → New Mapping

| Old Section (1961 Act) | New Section (2025 Act) | Subject | Change Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| 111A | **196** | Tax on STCG from equity (STT paid) | Renumbered only |
| 112 | **197** | Tax on LTCG from non-equity assets | Renumbered only |
| 112A | **198** | Tax on LTCG from equity (STT paid) | Renumbered only |

All three sections carry forward the rates as amended by Finance (No.2) Act 2024. The 2025 Act does not modify any rate, threshold, or grandfathering rule.

**Bottom line: 111A→196, 112→197, 112A→198. Update your section references. The rates are what FA 2024 set — the 2025 Act changes nothing here.**

---

## LTCL vs. STCG Set-Off — Proposed and Withdrawn

This is the genuine 2025 Act development in the capital gains rate/loss space.

The **Income Tax Bill 2025** (as introduced) proposed a one-time set-off of long-term capital loss (LTCL) against short-term capital gain (STCG). This would have been a departure from the longstanding rule that LTCL can only be set off against LTCG.

**The final Income Tax Act 2025 withdrew this provision.** The traditional rule continues without exception:

| Loss Type | Can Be Set Off Against | Changed by 2025 Act? |
|---|---|---|
| STCL | Both STCG and LTCG | No |
| LTCL | **LTCG only** — not against STCG | No — proposed relaxation was withdrawn |

Carry-forward for 8 assessment years, filing within due date required — all unchanged.

> **Watch out:** Some early commentary on the Income Tax Bill 2025 reported the LTCL-vs-STCG set-off as a confirmed change. It was withdrawn before the final Act. If clients or colleagues reference this, correct the record.

**Bottom line: LTCL still only against LTCG. The proposed relaxation did not make it into the final Act.**

---

## Always-STCG Categories — Cross-Reference

These categories are always short-term regardless of holding period and are taxed at slab rates (not the concessional rate under Section 196):

| Asset Type | Governing Section | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Depreciable assets (block of assets) | Section 74 | Slab rates |
| Debt mutual funds (specified MF, acquired after 1 April 2023) | Section 76 | Slab rates |
| Market-linked debentures | Section 76 | Slab rates |

These are not eligible for the concessional STCG rate under Section 196 because they are not equity instruments on which STT is paid.

**Bottom line: Section 74 and 76 assets remain always-STCG at slab rates. No change by the 2025 Act.**

---

## Capital Loss Set-Off — Practical Scenarios

| Scenario | Set-Off Available? |
|---|---|
| LTCL on equity vs. LTCG on real estate | **Yes** — both are long-term, cross-asset set-off permitted |
| LTCL on equity vs. STCG on equity | **No** — LTCL cannot be set off against STCG (proposed relaxation withdrawn) |
| STCL on equity vs. LTCG on equity | **Yes** — STCL can be set off against any capital gains |
| STCL on debt MF (Section 76) vs. LTCG on equity | **Yes** — Section 76 losses are STCL, settable against any capital gains |

## What You Must Update

1. **Section references in returns and computations:** 111A→196, 112→197, 112A→198.
2. **Client expectations on LTCL set-off:** If anyone assumed the LTCL-vs-STCG set-off would be available (based on the Bill), clarify that it was withdrawn.

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Sections 111A/112/112A → Sections 196/197/198.** All three rate sections renumbered. No rate or threshold change by the 2025 Act.
- **Rates are FA 2024 rates carried forward** — STCG equity 20% (Section 196), LTCG equity 12.5% above Rs 1.25 lakh (Section 198), LTCG other 12.5% without indexation (Section 197). All unchanged by the 2025 Act.
- **LTCL vs. STCG one-time set-off:** Proposed in Income Tax Bill 2025, **withdrawn** in the final Act. LTCL continues to be settable only against LTCG.
- **Always-STCG categories** (depreciable assets under Section 74, debt MFs/MLDs under Section 76) continue at slab rates — no change.
- **Capital loss carry-forward:** 8 assessment years, return within due date — unchanged.
