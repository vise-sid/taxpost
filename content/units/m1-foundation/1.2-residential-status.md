# Unit 1.2: Residential Status

## Overview

Section 6 (Residence in India) retains the same section number and all substantive tests are unchanged — the 182-day rule, 60+365 rule, 120-day rule, RNOR conditions, and POEM test remain identical. However, the internal sub-section structure has been significantly reorganized: old Section 6 had 6 sub-sections with multiple provisos; the new Section 6 has 13+ sub-sections with zero provisos. Every proviso has been converted to a standalone, independently citable sub-section. One minor language change exists: "for the purpose of employment outside India" is now "for employment outside India."

---

## Section 6: Sub-section Mapping

The section number is preserved (Section 6 → Section 6), but the internal sub-section numbers have changed due to proviso expansion.

| Provision | Old Reference | New Reference | Nature of Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic 182-day test | 6(1)(a) | **6(2)(a)** | Renumbered |
| Alternative 60+365 test | 6(1)(c) | **6(2)(b)** | Renumbered |
| Crew member relaxation | Proviso to 6(1) | **6(3)(a)** | Proviso → sub-section |
| Employment abroad relaxation | Proviso to 6(1) | **6(3)(b)** | Proviso → sub-section |
| Indian citizen/POI visiting (general) | Proviso to 6(1)(c) | **6(4)** | Proviso → sub-section |
| 120-day rule (income > ₹15 lakh) | Proviso to 6(1)(c) | **6(5)** | Proviso → sub-section |
| Deemed residence (stateless) | **6(1A)** | **6(7)** | Renumbered |
| HUF residence | 6(2) | **6(8)-(9)** | Renumbered |
| Firm/AOP/BOI residence | 6(3)-(4) | **6(10)-(11)** | Renumbered |
| Company POEM test | **6(5)** | **6(12)** | Renumbered |
| RNOR conditions | **6(6)** | **6(13)** | Renumbered |

### Critical Mappings

Three sub-section mappings are referenced most frequently in practice:

| Provision | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Deemed residence (stateless Indian citizens) | 6(1A) | **6(7)** |
| Company residence (POEM) | 6(5) | **6(12)** |
| RNOR conditions | 6(6) | **6(13)** |

---

## Structural Change: Provisos → Sub-sections

The old Section 6 buried relaxations for crew members, overseas employees, and high-income visitors as provisos within Section 6(1). The new Act extracts each into its own sub-section.

**Old structure:**
```
Section 6(1)(a) — 182-day rule
Section 6(1)(c) — 60+365 rule
  Proviso 1: Crew members and employment abroad...
  Proviso 2: Indian citizens/POI visiting from abroad...
  Proviso 3: High-income visitors (120-day threshold)...
```

**New structure:**
```
Section 6(2)(a) — 182-day rule
Section 6(2)(b) — 60+365 rule
Section 6(3)(a) — Crew member relaxation
Section 6(3)(b) — Employment abroad relaxation
Section 6(4) — Indian citizen/POI visiting relaxation
Section 6(5) — 120-day rule for high-income visitors
```

Each relaxation now has an independent address. Opinions and notices can cite specific sub-sections instead of referencing provisos within sub-sections.

---

## Language Change

One specific language modification:

| Old Act | New Act |
|---|---|
| "for the **purpose of** employment outside India" | "for employment outside India" |

This is characterized as language simplification with no intended change in legal effect. However, the deletion of "for the purpose of" could become relevant in interpretive disputes regarding the scope of the employment abroad relaxation. CBDT clarifications should be monitored.

---

## NRI Provisions: Section Mapping

The concessional tax regime for NRI investment income is preserved with new section numbers:

| Provision | Old Section | New Section | Nature of Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| NRI investment income definition | 115C | **213** | Renumbered only |
| Concessional rates (20% investment, 10-12.5% LTCG) | 115D-115E | **214** | Renumbered only |
| Reinvestment exemption (3-year lock-in) | 115F | **215** | Renumbered only |
| Filing exemption (investment + LTCG with TDS) | 115G | **216** | Renumbered only |
| Continuation election (returning NRI) | 115H | **217** | Renumbered only |
| NRE account interest exemption | Section 10 | **Schedule IV** | Moved to schedule |

All provisions are substantively identical — same rates, conditions, and lock-in periods.

---

## Transition: Lookback Calculations

The RNOR test under Section 6(13) requires looking back 10 preceding tax years (9-out-of-10 test) and 7 preceding tax years (729-day test). For Tax Year 2026-27, these lookback periods extend into years governed by the old Act.

**Section 536(3)** resolves this: a reference to "tax year" in the 2025 Act is construed as a reference to the corresponding "previous year" under the repealed Act for any year commencing on or before April 1, 2025. Lookback calculations work seamlessly across the transition.

## Confirmation: What Did NOT Change

| Rule | Status |
|---|---|
| 182-day test | Unchanged |
| 60+365 test (60 days + 365 in 4 preceding years) | Unchanged |
| 120-day rule for high-income visitors (₹15 lakh threshold) | Unchanged |
| Crew member CDC exclusion | Unchanged |
| Employment abroad → 182-day only test | Unchanged |
| Deemed residence (stateless, > ₹15 lakh, classified as RNOR) | Unchanged |
| RNOR: NR in 9 of 10 years OR ≤729 days in 7 years | Unchanged |
| POEM test for companies | Unchanged |
| HUF: control and management wholly outside India | Unchanged |
| Scope of income per residential status (Section 5) | Unchanged |
| NRI investment concessions | Unchanged (renumbered 115C-H → 213-217) |

---

## Practical Impact

- All opinions, notices, and letters citing Section 6 proviso references require updating to new sub-section numbers
- NRI advisory practice: references to Sections 115C-115H must be updated to 213-217
- RNOR analysis templates should reference Section 6(13) instead of 6(6)
- Deemed residence opinions should cite Section 6(7) instead of 6(1A)
- Residential status determination worksheets need updated section references for Tax Year 2026-27 onwards

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Section 6 → Section 6** — same section number, but internal sub-sections restructured from 6 + provisos to 13+ clean sub-sections
- **Three critical mappings**: 6(1A)→6(7) deemed residence, 6(5)→6(12) POEM, 6(6)→6(13) RNOR
- **Provisos → sub-sections** — every relaxation is now independently citable
- **One language change**: "for the purpose of employment" → "for employment" — monitor for interpretive impact
- **NRI provisions**: 115C-115H → 213-217 (renumbered only)
- **Section 536(3)** bridges lookback calculations across the old/new Act transition
- **Zero substantive changes** — all tests, thresholds, and categories are identical
