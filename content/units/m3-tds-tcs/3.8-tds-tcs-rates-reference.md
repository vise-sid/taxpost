# Unit 3.8: TDS/TCS Rates & Thresholds Quick Reference

## Overview

This unit serves as a consolidated reference card for all TDS and TCS rates, thresholds, and section mappings under the Income Tax Act, 2025. It is designed for quick lookup during return preparation, certificate issuance, and advisory work. Every rate band, every threshold, and every old-to-new section mapping is collected here in one place.

## TCS Rates: Old vs New (Finance Bill 2026)

| Sl. No. | Nature | Old Rate | New Rate | Direction | Threshold |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Alcoholic liquor | 1% | **2%** | Increased | Nil |
| 2 | Tendu leaves | 5% | **2%** | Decreased | Nil |
| 3 | Timber / forest produce | 2.5% | **2%** | Decreased | Nil |
| 4 | Scrap | 1% | **2%** | Increased | Nil |
| 5 | Coal / lignite / iron ore | 1% | **2%** | Increased | Nil |
| 6 | Motor vehicle | 1% | **1%** | Unchanged | Rs 10 lakh |
| 7(a) | LRS — education / medical | 5% | **2%** | Decreased | Rs 10 lakh |
| 7(b) | LRS — other purposes | 20% | **20%** | Unchanged | Rs 10 lakh |
| 8 | Overseas tour package | 5%/20% | **2%** | Decreased | Removed |
| 9 | Parking / toll / mining | 2% | **2%** | Unchanged | Nil |

**Summary:** 5 entries moved to 2% (from 1%, 2.5%, or 5%), 2 entries unchanged at existing rates, 1 entry simplified from tiered to flat 2%, and LRS-other remains at 20%.

## Key Takeaways

- **TDS rates span seven bands**: 0.1%, 1%, 2%, 5%, 10%, 20%, and 30% — plus slab rates for salary under Section 392.
- **30% applies exclusively to speculative/gaming income**: lottery, online gaming, and horse race winnings (Table 3, Sl. 1–3).
- **10% is the most common rate band**, covering professional fees, interest, dividends, mutual fund income, NSS, and partner payments.
- **2% covers contractor payments, rent (individual/HUF), e-commerce, cash withdrawal, and lottery commission** — a diverse set of transactions.
- **TCS rates have been rationalized under Finance Bill 2026**: most rates converge to 2%, with motor vehicle at 1% and LRS-other at 20% as the two outliers.
- **Old section numbers map cleanly to new table serial numbers** — the cross-reference table above provides the complete mapping for citation purposes.
- **25 distinct thresholds** exist across TDS/TCS provisions — ranging from Rs 2,500 (NSS) to Rs 3 crore (cash withdrawal for co-ops).
- **14 new form numbers** replace the old compliance forms — memorize Forms 130, 138, 140, 141, and 143 as the five most frequently used.
