# Definitions — Section 2 of Income Tax Act 2025

## Administrative/Official Terms
- **Assessing Officer**: ITO/ADIT/ADC with relevant jurisdiction
- **Commissioner**: Person appointed as Commissioner of Income-tax
- **Board**: Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) under 1963 Act
- **Appellate Tribunal**: Tribunal constituted under section 361

## Financial/Tax Terms
- **Advance Tax**: Payable per Chapter XIX-C
- **Income**: Profits and gains; dividend; voluntary contributions; perquisites; VDA income
- **Total Income**: Total amount computed per section 5
- **Capital Asset**: Property held by assessee, excluding stock-in-trade and personal effects (except jewelry >Rs 10L)
- **Agricultural Income**: Rent/revenue from Indian agricultural land; produce processing; nursery crops
- **Dividend**: Distribution by company of accumulated profits or assets
- **Tax Year**: 12-month period April 1 to March 31 (Section 3) — replaces Previous Year concept

## Entity Types
- **Company**: Indian company, foreign corporation, or Board-declared institution
- **Domestic Company**: Indian company or foreign company with prescribed dividend arrangements
- **Person**: Individual, HUF, company, firm, AOP, BOI, local authority, artificial juridical person
- **Assessee**: Person liable for tax; includes those in assessment proceedings
- **Firm**: Per Indian Partnership Act 1932; includes LLP

## Property/Asset Terms
- **Transfer**: Sale, exchange, relinquishment, extinction of rights, conversion to stock-in-trade
- **Short-term Capital Asset**: Held ≤24 months (≤12 months for listed shares/equity MF/zero coupon bonds)
- **Long-term Capital Asset**: Capital asset that is NOT short-term
- **Block of Assets**: Group within asset class at same depreciation rate
- **Fair Market Value**: Price ordinarily fetched in open market

## Business Terms
- **Business**: Trade, commerce, manufacture, or commercial adventure
- **Profession**: Includes vocation
- **Manufacture**: Physical transformation creating distinct object
- **Slump Sale**: Transfer of undertaking for lump sum without individual asset values

## Specialized Entity Definitions
- **Amalgamation**: Merger of companies with property/liability transfer and shareholder continuity
- **Demerger**: Undertaking transfer via scheme with asset/liability shift
- **Business Trust**: InvIT or REIT under SEBI regulations
- **Partnership/Firm**: Per Indian Partnership Act 1932; includes LLP

## Financial Instruments
- **Interest**: Payment for borrowed money/debt, including service fees
- **Zero Coupon Bond**: Infrastructure/public sector bond with no pre-maturity payments
- **Securities**: Per Securities Contracts (Regulation) Act 1956
- **Virtual Digital Asset (VDA)**: Cryptographic code/token representing value, including crypto-assets and NFTs

## Residency Terms
- **Resident**: Person resident in India per section 6
- **Non-resident**: Not a resident
- **Not Ordinarily Resident (NOR)**: Resident who was non-resident in 9 of 10 preceding years or was in India ≤729 days in 7 preceding years
- **Senior Citizen**: Individual aged 60+ during tax year

## Other Key Terms
- **Relative**: Spouse, siblings, ascendants, descendants
- **Document**: Includes electronic records per IT Act 2000
- **PAN**: Ten-character alphanumeric identifier
- **Prescribed**: Determined by rules under this Act

## Key Changes from Old Act (Section 2 of 1961)
1. Definitions simplified without changing meaning
2. Repeated definitions consolidated into Section 2
3. Virtual Digital Asset formally defined
4. "Tax Year" replaces "Previous Year" + "Assessment Year"
5. Total number of definitions reduced through consolidation
