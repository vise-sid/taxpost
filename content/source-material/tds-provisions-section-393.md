# TDS Provisions — Section 392-393 of Income Tax Act 2025

Effective: April 1, 2026
Replaces: Sections 192-196D of Income Tax Act 1961

## Overview
- Section 392: TDS on Salary
- Section 393: TDS on non-salary payments (consolidated)
- Section 394: TCS (Tax Collected at Source)
- Approx 37 old TDS sections consolidated into ~20 provisions

## Section 392: TDS on Salary

| Head | Threshold | Rate | Old Section |
|---|---|---|---|
| Salary Income | Nil | Per applicable slab rates | 192 |
| EPF/Recognised PF Withdrawal | Rs 50,000 | 10% | 192A |

## Key Changes from Old Act
1. Consolidation: 60+ scattered TDS sections → 3 organized tables
2. Structure: Organized by payee type (Resident / Non-Resident / Any Person)
3. Rates: Largely unchanged — primarily renumbering and reorganization
4. Thresholds: Most thresholds retained from 1961 Act

## TDS Compliance
- TAN required for deductors
- Quarterly TDS returns (Form 26Q equivalent under new rules)
- TDS certificates (Form 16/16A equivalents under Income Tax Rules 2026)
