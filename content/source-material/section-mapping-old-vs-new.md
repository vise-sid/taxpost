# Income Tax Act 1961 vs 2025 — Section Mapping

## Structural Overview
- Old Act (1961): 700+ sections, 823 pages
- New Act (2025): 536 sections, 622 pages, 23 chapters, 16 schedules
- Effective: April 1, 2026
- Key concept change: "Previous Year + Assessment Year" → single "Tax Year"

## Tax Slabs — New Regime (Section 202)

| Income Range | Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to Rs 4 lakh | NIL |
| Rs 4-8 lakh | 5% |
| Rs 8-12 lakh | 10% |
| Rs 12-16 lakh | 15% |
| Rs 16-20 lakh | 20% |
| Rs 20-24 lakh | 25% |
| Above Rs 24 lakh | 30% |

Rebate: Rs 60,000 for income up to Rs 12 lakh (new regime)
